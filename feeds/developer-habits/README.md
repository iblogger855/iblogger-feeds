## 💡 Common Questions

**Q: Should I share productivity systems even if they don't work for everyone?**  
A: Yes! Different systems work for different people. Share what works for you with caveats.

**Q: Is this about becoming a "10x developer"?**  
A: No. This is about sustainable, healthy growth and improvement.

**Q: Can I write about struggling with productivity?**  
A: Absolutely! Sharing challenges is as valuable as sharing solutions.
