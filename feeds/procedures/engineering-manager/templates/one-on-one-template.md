# Template: 1-on-1 Notes (Engineering Manager)

> **How to use:** Keep one running doc per direct report (append each session at the top). The 1-on-1 is your core craft as an EM — it belongs to *them*, not to you, so let them drive. Keep these notes private and growth-focused; following through on what you capture here is how trust is built. The discovery questions are especially useful in your first weeks. ## ─── FIRST-MEETING DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (new EM) ───────────────────

> Ask these of every direct report in your first two weeks. Same questions for each — patterns across answers reveal the real state of the team. Listen 80%, promise nothing.

- What's working well that I should **not** change?
- What's the most frustrating part of your week?
- Where do you want to grow — and do you feel like you are right now?
- If you were in my seat, what's the first thing you'd fix?
- How do you like to get feedback and recognition?
- What does a great manager do for you? What did the last one get wrong?
- Is there anything making you think about leaving?
