# Template: Individual Growth Plan (Engineering Manager)

> **How to use:** A forward-looking development plan you co-author *with* a report — see [04 — Performance & Growth](../04-performance-and-growth.md). Capture where they are, where they want to go, the gap, concrete actions, the support you'll provide, and check-in dates. Revisit it in [1-on-1s](../03-one-on-ones-and-feedback.md) — a plan written once and never reopened is theater. This same structure works for a support plan when someone is struggling: same partnership, same honesty, same goal — help them succeed, with no surprises.

# Growth Plan — Rotha · 2026-07-06

**Manager:** Mony
**Current level:** Mid-level engineer
**Plan type:** [x] Support (closing a gap)  ·  [ ] Development

> Context: Rotha has been underdelivering for a couple of quarters, but no one had ever told him clearly. Mony gave direct, kind SBI feedback in 1-on-1s over the first month so this plan is not a surprise — it is a real, supported chance to succeed, co-authored with Rotha.

## 🧭 Where this is going
- **Direction:** get back to solidly meeting mid-level expectations, then resume growth.
- **Why it matters to him:** Rotha said he'd lost confidence after a rough project and felt set adrift when the senior left; he wants to feel effective again.

## 📐 The gap (current → target)
| Area | Today | Target |
|:-----|:------|:-------|
| Task completion | Stories slip repeatedly; estimates unreliable | Reliably finishes committed work, flags risk early |
| Code quality | Frequent rework after review | PRs merge with minor comments |
| Communication | Goes quiet when stuck | Asks for help within a day of being blocked |

## 🎬 Concrete actions
- [ ] Pair with Dara twice a week on the billing epic to rebuild technical confidence
- [ ] Take one well-scoped story per sprint to completion before adding more
- [ ] Daily async check-in note in our channel: what's done, what's blocked

## 🤝 Support I'll provide
- Weekly (not biweekly) 1-on-1s for the duration; pairing time with Dara protected; reduced parallel load so he can focus; clear, same-day feedback.

## 📅 Check-in dates
- 2026-07-20 — first two weeks: is the pairing helping? early signal on completion.
- 2026-08-17 — 6-week review: clear, honest read on progress against the gaps; involve HR/Vibol if not improving, to ensure fairness.

> Agreed and signed by both. Expectations are explicit; we review honestly at each date — no surprises, real support, a fair chance.
