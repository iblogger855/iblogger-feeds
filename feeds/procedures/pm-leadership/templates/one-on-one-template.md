## ─── FIRST-MEETING DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (new PM) ───────────────────

> Ask these of every team member and key stakeholder in your first two weeks. Same questions for each — patterns across answers reveal the real problems.

- What's working well that I should **not** change?
- What slows you down or wastes your time each week?
- If you were in my seat, what's the first thing you'd fix?
- What information do you wish you had that you don't?
- How do you like to be communicated with (and how often)?
- What does "this project is going well" look like to you?
