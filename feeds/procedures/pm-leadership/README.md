# 📋 PM Leadership Playbook

A practical, step-by-step playbook for someone stepping into a **Project Manager role for the first time, in a new company/workspace.** (Delivery/Agile PM focus.) It covers both halves of the job:

1. **Onboarding** — what *you* do in your first 90 days to land well.
2. **Process** — the planning, cadence, reporting, and risk artifacts you *produce* for the project.

> Built on one principle: **a PM leads through influence, not authority.** Your power comes from making the goal clear, the path visible, and the obstacles disappear — so spend your first month earning trust and understanding the delivery system before you change it.

Each procedure follows the house format: a **Mermaid swimlane diagram**, a portable **ASCII flow**, and a **step-by-step responsibility table** — so it works in GitHub, Confluence, or a terminal.

## 🧭 How to Use This Playbook

```
You just got the PM role
        │
        ▼
  Read 01 (First 90 Days) ── fill the 30/60/90 template
        │
        ▼
  Days 1–14:  LISTEN  → 1-on-1 template, discovery questions
  Days 15–30: ASSESS  → 02 + Delivery Assessment template
  Days 31–60: PLAN    → 03 (Planning & Estimation) + roadmap
  Days 61–90: EXECUTE → 04, 05, 06 + status report + risk log
```

> **New to the role and short on time?** Read **01** today, skim **02**, and start your 1-on-1s tomorrow. Everything else can wait until you understand the team.
