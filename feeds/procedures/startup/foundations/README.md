# 🌱 Startup Foundations

The *mindset* layer of starting a business — the thinking that comes **before** the architecture, the MVP, and the code. Frameworks and lessons (often written up from talks, books, and operators) on how to decide whether and what to build.

> Where the [clone playbooks](../clone/README.md) and [market-expansion guide](../01-africa-market-expansion-architecture.md) cover *how to build and scale*, this folder covers *why, what, and whether* — the foundations a founder needs in place first.
