# MBA Public Administration — Robert Kennedy College

**Major:** Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Public Administration  
**College:** Robert Kennedy College (in partnership with University of Cumbria, UK) 🇨🇭🇬🇧  
**Structure:** 1-year duration · 100% Online  

The Online MBA Public Administration is designed to equip professionals with the strategic, analytical, and ethical skills needed to excel in today’s public sector. Unlike a traditional MPA that focuses mainly on internal government processes, this MBA blends essential business management disciplines (strategy, analytics, leadership) with a tailored public-sector focus. This ensures graduates gain versatile, results-oriented skills that drive efficiency, innovation, and impact across governance and public administration.
