# 🏫 Robert Kennedy College (RKC)

**Type:** Private Educational Institution (Online)  
**Location:** Zürich, Switzerland 🇨🇭  
**Partnerships:** University of Cumbria, University of Salford, University of Gloucestershire (UK)  
**Website:** rkc.edu / rkc.swiss  
**Structure:** 100% Online, typically 1-year Master's/MBA programs  

Robert Kennedy College (RKC) is a private educational institution in Zürich, Switzerland, offering highly flexible, rigorous online master's programs in exclusive partnership with British universities. Its model is designed for working professionals globally, combining Swiss quality education with UK-accredited degrees.
