# Template: KYC — Accommodation Provider (Hotel / Guesthouse / Property Business)

**Tags:** #template #kyc #kyb #hotel #accommodation #booking #property-business  
**Use When:** Onboarding a hotel, guesthouse, B&B, or accommodation business onto a booking platform (Booking.com-style)  
**Read Time:** ~5 min

> This template covers KYB (Know Your Business) verification for accommodation providers — businesses, not individuals. It handles company identity, property portfolio, rate/availability contract, bank details, and regulatory licenses. Applies to hotels, resorts, guesthouses, serviced apartments, and hostel chains.

## Property Tier Reference

| Tier | Examples | KYB Depth | Inspection Required |
|:-----|:---------|:---------|:-------------------|
| **Individual / Micro** | 1–5 rooms, owner-operated | Identity + license only | Self-certification photos |
| **Small independent** | 6–30 rooms, single property | Full KYB + UBO + license | Virtual photo review |
| **Mid-size hotel** | 31–150 rooms | Full KYB + UBO + EDD if flagged | In-person optional |
| **Large hotel / resort** | 150+ rooms | Full KYB + UBO + EDD | Third-party audit |
| **Hotel chain** | Multiple properties | Corporate KYB once + per-property license | Sample audit |
