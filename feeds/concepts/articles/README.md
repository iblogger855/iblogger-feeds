## 🛠️ Contribution Guidelines (ការណែនាំសម្រាប់ការចូលរួមសរសេរ)

To maintain structural integrity and a premium reading experience, all new concept articles must adhere to the following standard template (Gold Standard established by `02-five-whys-technique.md`):

1. **Title & Frontmatter**: Descriptive bilingual title, author, date, and HSL-styled tag metadata.
2. **Introduction (លំនាំបញ្ហា)**: A highly engaging, real-world narrative or analogy in Khmer.
3. **Core Explanation (១. បញ្ហា)**: A deep technical/psychological breakdown of the concept.
4. **Real World Examples (២. ឧទាហរណ៍ជាក់ស្តែងក្នុងពិភពពិត)**:
   * At least **5 comprehensive software/team engineering examples** in fluent Khmer.
   * Each example must include a structured **5 Whys table** translating root causes.
   * Each example must have a **dedicated HSL-colored Mermaid flowchart** with labels wrapped to **2-3 words per line** using `<br/>` to prevent clipping.
5. **The Aggravator (៣. កត្តាជម្រុញ)**: Catalysts and systemic factors that worsen the issue.
6. **The General Solution (៤. ដំណោះស្រាយទូទៅ)**: Structured, highly actionable guidelines for teams.
7. **Conclusion (សេចក្តីសន្និដ្ឋាន)**: Synthesis of the takeaways.
8. **Khmer Spelling Compliance**: Ensure the word for "Example" is strictly spelled **`ឧទាហរណ៍`** (using the letter `រ` (Ro), NOT `ម` (Mo)).
9. **Anchor Redirection Check**: Always run the automated unicode-aware slugification script (`fix_toc.py`) to align Table of Contents anchors with header changes.
